Kashyyk

Once ruled by the Great Wookie tribes, the forest planet of Kashyyk, like so many other planets, has fallen to the Sith Empire's relentless crusade. Now goverened by a mega corporation only interested in plundering the planet of it's natural resources, Wookies are forced to bend the knee of servitude less they experience the Sith Empire's wrath.


But amongst the tribes there are stirrings of rebellion that, once ignited, may ascend the high treetops of Kashyyk and see the Wookies retake their place as the planet's rightful guardians.

Dantooine

Lush and green, the Earth-like Dantooine is a shining beacon of peace and tranquility in a galaxy devoured by war. Home to a Jedi Temple and seat of the Jedi Council, the relative serenity of Dantooine is in stark contrast to the chaos and destruction being wrecked on some of it's close neighbours.


But no planet is perfect and while war is not openly waged on Dantooine there are still conflicts to be dealt with. Ancient families who call Dantooine their home feud over resources and secret lovers in the families only feed fuel to the fire. Elsewhere, Kyber Crystals, used to create lightsabers, are keenly sought after by wannabe Jedi and looters alike, but be warned, the caves in which they dwell are not always empty.

Korriban

Once an Ancient Sith stronghold, Korriban fell to ruins following the Jedi's erradication of the Sith. But with a new Empire and many hungry young Sith eager to seek out power, Korriban has once more been occupied by the Sith and an academy built there to test those who wish to join the Sith ranks.

But where Jedi are taught to harness the Force through peace of mind and self discipline, the Sith are taught but one, the pursuit of power trumps all else. There is no place for the weak amongst the Sith, only power and domination will allow you to succeed on Korriban.

Taris

Rise to the top or fall to the bottom, there is no middle ground on Taris. Work your way to the top and stay there is the dream of every Taris resident. This philosophy is visible in the very structure of the planet. Cities are divided into three areas, the Upper City, home to the city's wealty and well off, the Lower City, populated by the city's no-goods, and the Underground, home to the rats, sewers and anyone the people of Taris deem to be on the same level as them.

Taris and it's doctrine of power was attractive to the Sith and the people of Taris were all to keen to welcome their new invaders and get away from the endless beaurocracy that seemed to plague the Republic. Keep your friends close and enemies closer on Taris.
