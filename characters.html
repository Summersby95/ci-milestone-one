Bastilla Shan

Powerful Force user, Hero of the Republic, Battle Commander, Bastilla Shan has many titles and accomplished much but, much to her dismay, the Sith Empire remains at large. Sent on a daring mission to execute the head of the snake, Darth Revan, she completes her mission only to find the head replaced by the equally despicable Darth Malak.


Bitter and defeated after her apparent failure, Bastilla, is losing faith in the Republic's cause and her fellow Jedi. On a new mission now to recover fragments of a Star Map which could lead to Darth Malk and an end to this bitter war, Bastilla is at a crossroads and the player's interactions with her will determine the path she takes.

Carth Onasi

Expert Republic pilot Carth Onasi is no stranger to battle or victory. However in the one fight he was needed most he failed and with that failure came the loss of his wife and son at the hands of Sith Empire. Hellbent on revenge, Carth won't stop until the Sith Empire is eradicated.


Carth's struggle to reconcile his anger about the loss of his family and his hatred of the new Sith Empire that he inadvertedly helped create is central to the plot of KoToR.

Darth Malak

Once a hero of the Republic, Darth Malak and his master Darth Malak fell from the light after wading into the Mandalorian War alongside the Republic against the will of the Jedi Council. Having committed countless atrocities in their fight to wrest control of the Outer Rim from the Mandalorians the two Jedi as well as their followers fell to the dark side after uncovering an ancient temple at the edge of the galaxy.


With his former master out of the picture, Malak now rules the Sith Empire with an Iron Fist and after uncovering a Star Map that leads to an unimaginably powerful weapon, he finally has the tools to wreck havok on the Galaxy. Only the player and his allies have the power to stop him.

HK-47

While most droids are programmed to help their masters in menial tasks like repairing ships, healing people, moving things, HK-47 knows only one command: Kill.


With his previous owner gone, HK wants only to help his new master unleash death and destruction on whoever is unfortunate enough to cross their path.
