What Is Knights of The Old Republic?

Knights of The Old Republic is a role-playing game set in the Star Wars Universe developed by BioWare and published by LucasArts in 2003. Set 4'000 years before the formation of the Galactic Empire, Darth Malak, a Dark Lord of the Sith has unleashed a Sith armada against the Galactic Republic. The player must venture to different planets in the galaxy to defeat Malak, along the way deciding the fate's of peoples of the planets they visit and, ultimately, the galaxy as a whole. As the player makes decisions they grow closer to the Light or Dark Side of the force, ultimately deciding their role as a Jedi or Sith.


Knights of The Old Republic changed the landscape for role-playing games setting new standards in game mechanics, role-playing mechanics, player agency in story development and character depth. It received critical acclaim upon release and is often cited as one of the best video games ever made. It has inspired numerous role-playing game franchises like Mass Effect, Fallout, The Elder Scrolls and Pillars of Eternity.

Why Does It Need A Remaster?

While revolutionary at the time, Knights of the Old Republic has not aged well. It's graphics in particular fall far short of modern players' expectations for games. As well as this, the core gameplay mechanics are dated and often clunky compared to modern games and have been improved upon by many of the games it helped inspire.


These factors deter new fans from experiencing and old fans from revisiting this classic because the overall experience pales in comparison to modern games. With so many classic games being remastered of late, it feels like the perfect time for a developer to take a swing at remastering this masterpiece!
